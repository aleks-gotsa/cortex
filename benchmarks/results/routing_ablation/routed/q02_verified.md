# Executive Summary

GPT-4 and Claude are two advanced language models developed by different companies, each with unique capabilities and training methodologies. GPT-4, released in March 2023, is a multimodal model capable of processing both text and images, while Claude, also from 2023, focuses on ethical AI development through 'constitutional AI.' Key differences include their architecture, training data, and specific capabilities.

GPT-4 has demonstrated improved performance across reasoning, retention, and coding tasks compared to its predecessors. However, it has faced criticism for generating inappropriate content in certain contexts. Claude, on the other hand, is designed with ethical considerations at the forefront, using 'constitutional AI' techniques to enhance compliance. Both models have been tested for potential misuse, but GPT-4's performance in specific tasks like coding and web search has shown significant improvements over previous versions.

# Key Differences

## Architecture
GPT-4 [2] is a multimodal model capable of processing both text and images as input, whereas Claude [1] primarily focuses on text-based interactions. This multimodal capability allows GPT-4 to provide more versatile responses based on visual inputs, making it suitable for applications requiring image analysis or context from images.

## Training Data
GPT-4 is trained using a large corpus of books and web data, similar to its predecessors [5]. Claude, however, uses 'constitutional AI' techniques that prioritize ethical and legal compliance during training. This approach ensures that Claude's responses are more aligned with societal norms and values, reducing the risk of generating harmful or unethical content.

## Key Capabilities
GPT-4 has shown significant improvements in coding tasks, web search capabilities, and general text generation [5]. It can perform complex reasoning and retain information over extended conversations. Claude, while also capable of these tasks, places a stronger emphasis on ethical considerations and compliance with legal standards [1].

## Performance Comparison
In specific tasks such as coding and web search, GPT-4 has demonstrated superior performance compared to Claude [2][5]. However, Claude's focus on ethical AI development suggests that its responses are more reliable in terms of avoiding harmful content.

# Open Questions
